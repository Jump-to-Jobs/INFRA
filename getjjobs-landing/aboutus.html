Our Story: The Genesis of JJobs

JJobs was born from a simple belief: hiring should be human, not a hurdle. We're transforming stressful job hunting into a fun, authentic experience.

See Our Solution

"We believe the problem isn't with students it's with outdated systems. JJobs helps both sides connect in a more human way."

- Team JJobs

Our Journey So Far

Key milestones that define our path to revolutionizing recruitment.

2025 Q1

Startup Weekend Wellington 2025 Winner

JJobs secures first place, validating the urgent need for a better system for both students and employers. This initial success fueled our mission.

2025 Q2

First Paying Employer Partnership

Our first company partner joins, proving that employers are ready to invest in a more efficient and authentic hiring process. This marked a significant step in our journey.

2025 Q3

App Beta Launch & Community Growth

The initial version of JJobs goes live, successfully connecting students with opportunities through innovative video profiles. Our community begins to grow rapidly.

2025 Q4

Expanding Across Aotearoa (New Zealand)

We continue to expand our reach, bringing video-based hiring to more regions and industries across New Zealand, empowering more job seekers and employers.
